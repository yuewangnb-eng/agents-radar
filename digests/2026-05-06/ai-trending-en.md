**Local-First Resurgence** appears in `local-deep-research` (Qwen3.6-27B on consumer GPU), `ollama`'s expanded Chinese model support, and `CowAgent`'s OS-level integration. This isn't mere privacy theater; it's cost optimization as frontier API pricing stabilizes at premium tiers and developers seek predictable infrastructure spend.

**Chinese Model Integration** is now table stakes: DeepSeek, Qwen, Kimi, GLM, MiniMax all appear in tooling defaults, not as afterthoughts. The geopolitical decoupling of AI stacks hasn't occurred; instead, developers treat strong Chinese models as interchangeable infrastructure components.
